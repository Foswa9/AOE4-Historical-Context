How do you play as the HRE


The game can be split into the different ages aswell as either in offence state or defence state.
HRE shine in the castle age and imperial age however it is still possible to win in earlier ages as can be seen by the pro players however simply out microing and macroring your opponent is usualy this tactic is possible
There are 3 ways to win the game. Playing as the HRE will affect the way you achieve these goals
1.Landmark victory, Defeat the enemy landmark is very obvious. The difficulty will be how to beat the enemies army who will be defending, while simultanously defending your own base + growing and protecting eco , Although there is a lot of creativity involved, which is why this game is so beautful , however their are a few principles that will help you.
The HRE most defining feature is their infantry. Their MAA 1. can be trained in the feudal age and 2. have unique upgrades. They also have a unique unit known as the landknecht.
Another defining feauture is their religious unit known as the prelate. In their monastery/cathederal building they have access unique upgrades. "Name goes here" acts as a permanent buff to all units when in the aura of the religious unit.



Unit composition: A core concept of unit composition is building counters to what your opponent is building. So its wise to understand what unit counters what
The foundation rock,paper & scissor system:
Ranged counters Spearmen
Spearmen counter calvalry
Cavalry counters ranged
Gunpowder ranged & Crossbow counters heavy armoured units though are weak so should be protected.
All cavalry counter ranged units
Horsemen => Archers & crossbow men
Knights => Archers & crossbow men
MAA => have no obvious counter but as they are armoured so are not counter by archers
